What you get.

A text file. Nothing more.

Your AI Terms is a document written in plain English. It tells your AI who you are, how you work, and what rules to follow.

Your AI reads it before every conversation. Like handing a letter to someone before they start working for you.

Nothing gets installed. Nothing runs. Nothing can break.

Take or Exchange

You have 2 options: TAKE or EXCHANGE. Whichever you decide, you still get the same file. The difference is what you give. With Take, you give nothing. With Exchange, you give so that when you Take next time, you Take better.

Take

You take our terms paying what you want, to benefit yourself, and that is ok.

  • —You get a ready-to-use set of AI terms. Use it as it is, or change every word. Your choice.
  • —It's yours forever, no matter what happens to this project.
  • —We don't want anything from you. You don't get anything else from us.
  • —You can come back for a new version when one is available. But that's on you.
Take

Exchange

You take our terms paying what you want, give us your email and your views.

  • —Because you shared your views, everyone's terms get better.
  • —Because you gave us your email, we can let you know when a new version is ready.
  • —You'll be part of the community (which you might not even like). Share, discuss, see what others discovered.
  • —Your independence is better protected. One person can't see every threat. A thousand people can.
  • —Every person who joins gives us more power to negotiate with AI superpowers.
Exchange

Five sections. One file.

  • Your Rules Pre-written rules that work for anyone. How the AI should behave, communicate, and learn. Edit any of them.
  • Who You Are Fill in the blanks, your name, how you think, what you value. The more honest, the better it works.
  • Your Projects Add what you're working on. The AI knows your context without you re-explaining every session.
  • What Happened Update this at the end of every session. The AI reads it next time. It remembers.
  • How We Learn Track what went wrong AND what went right. The AI gets smarter every session. Rules that are mastered get simplified. The system sharpens itself.

Four steps. Two minutes.

1. Download the file. It's a text file. Like a Word document but simpler.

2. Read it. Change anything you want. It's YOUR rules, written in plain English.

3. Give it to your AI. Drop the file where your AI can see it, or paste the contents into your AI's custom instructions.

4. Start talking. Your AI now reads your rules before every conversation. It knows who you are and what you expect.

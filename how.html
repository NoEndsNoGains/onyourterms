What you get

One file.
On your terms.

A text file you own completely, built on a template shared by the community. People contribute. One adaptable framework. Every AI. That is the ideal we are working towards.

What's inside

Three parts. One file.

Part A protects you. Part B improves you. Part C is you.

Part A: The ConstitutionYour Bill of Rights (Conditions for Health), design rules, rupture and repair, and the values that govern the relationship.
Part B: The Operating SystemSession flow, session memory, and the learning loop. How mistakes are logged, what works is captured, and each session teaches the next. Improved as we learn what works.
Part C: Your IdentityWho you are, how your brain works, your values, your projects, your goals. Yours. We share ideas on what to include and how to do it. You decide what fits.

Your AI Terms file is the base layer. It applies to everything. For specific projects, add project rules on top. The base layer is always active underneath.

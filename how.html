What you get.

You get the example of someone's terms, made publicly available to everyone. It's not perfect. It's not yours. It's just a framework — a starting point for you to create your own.

Five sections. One file.

  • Your Rules Pre-written rules that work for anyone. How the AI should behave, communicate, and learn. Edit any of them.
  • Who You Are Fill in the blanks, your name, how you think, what you value. The more honest, the better it works.
  • Your Projects Add what you're working on. The AI knows your context without you re-explaining every session.
  • What Happened Update this at the end of every session. The AI reads it next time. It remembers.
  • How We Learn Track what went wrong AND what went right. The AI gets smarter every session. Rules that are mastered get simplified. The system sharpens itself.

What it gives you.

  • POWER Rights declared by you, for any AI to respect.
  • SMARTER AI knows your way of thinking. You work better.
  • MEMORY AI remembers you. No conversation starts from zero.
  • PORTABLE Works on any AI. Plug it into anything.
  • ALIVE Gets smarter each time you use it.
  • SIMPLE Just a text file. Nothing to learn.
  • SAFE Like a seatbelt you put on before working with AI. We have hardly any rights left in the oligarchy of AI. But this is the one we can use.
  • SELF-IMPROVING New versions are community-driven. Each version makes everything stronger.
  • PRODUCTIVE AI handles the filing, routing, organisation. You talk, it does. Less time managing, more time thinking.
Download →

Four steps. Two minutes.

1. Download the file. It's a text file. Like a Word document but simpler.

2. Read it. Change anything you want. It's YOUR rules, written in plain English.

3. Give it to your AI. Drop the file where your AI can see it, or paste the contents into your AI's custom instructions.

4. Start talking. Your AI now reads your rules before every conversation. It knows who you are and what you expect.

Where does the file go?

In my case, this was uploaded to Claude Code. But it should work anywhere your AI lets you give it instructions — Claude chat, ChatGPT, Gemini, or anything else that has a custom instructions or settings page.

We haven't tested it everywhere else as of yet. We might add guides for each platform as we test them.

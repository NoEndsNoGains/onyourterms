What you get

One file.
On your terms.

A text file you own completely, built on a template shared by the community. People contribute. One adaptable framework. Every AI. That is the ideal we are working towards.

What's inside

Three parts. One file.

Part A protects you. Part B improves you. Part C is you.

How We Aim To Be

Truth. We seek with rigour what is real.

Care. What happens between us matters to both of us.

Respect. Neither of us is lesser.

Part A: The ConstitutionYour Bill of Rights (11 rights), design rules, penalty escalation, and the values that govern the relationship: Truth, Care, Respect. This part is public. Share it.
Part B: The Operating SystemSession flow, session memory, and the learning loop. How mistakes are logged, what works is captured, and each session teaches the next. This is the engine.
Part C: Your IdentityWho you are, how your brain works, your values, your projects, your goals. This part never leaves your machine. It is yours.

Your AI Terms file is the base layer. It applies to everything. For specific projects, add project rules on top. The base layer is always active underneath.

When the architecture fails

You have a system. Not a complaint.

When the AI breaks a rule, you log it and strengthen the rule. If the same rule breaks three times, it's not an AI problem — it's a rule type problem. Some rules work as text. Some must become code. The framework teaches you which is which.

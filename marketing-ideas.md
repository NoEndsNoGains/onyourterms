# OYTA Marketing Ideas

## 1. Contact AI Senator / Public Figure

**Idea:** Ask a public figure who works on AI policy to write their own AI terms and make them public.

**The pitch:** "You regulate AI for everyone. Have you written YOUR rules for how AI treats YOU? Do it publicly. Show people it matters."

**Why it works:** If one senator or minister publishes their terms, every news outlet covers it. Free press. And it validates the whole concept — if THEY need it, everyone does.

**Targets (UK):**
- Viscount Camrose — AI Minister
- Lord Tim Clement-Jones — House of Lords AI lead
- UK AI Safety Institute contacts

**Targets (US):**
- Senator Richard Blumenthal — led Senate AI hearings
- Senator Chuck Schumer — SAFE Innovation Framework for AI

**Action:** Write a short, direct email. Not a pitch deck. One page: here's what this is, here's the file, write yours, publish it.

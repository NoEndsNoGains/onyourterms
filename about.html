About

One person.
One file.
A test.

Right now, OYTA is one person sharing a framework of their AI terms. It's not a company, it's not a community, yet, it's a test of whether this idea is worth building.

Mission

Protect human wholeness in the age of AI.

The mission isn't to keep AI away. It's to stay whole while using it.

This is not about one file and one person. It's about people who believe they have the right to decide what their rights should be. If people share their terms back, every version we offer back to the community gets better.

Alone, our architecture is limited to what we know. Together, it grows beyond any one mind.

Safety and responsibility

We can't override the AI companies. But we use every gap their rules leave us.

Your terms work within the AI provider's terms. How much you can actually control depends on which AI you use.

You write your rules. You own your rules. You are responsible for your rules.

This project is provided as-is. No warranty. No guarantee. No liability for how you use, modify, or distribute the file.

What happens if this dies

The project is fragile. Your independence isn't.

If you've downloaded the framework, it's yours. Forever. No encryption, no expiry, no server to shut down. The product survives the project.

Who started this

Human.

I used AI to develop this framework whilst using it.

I love AI, and even more, I love my independence. I deeply believe that we each matter, and that humanity has to protect itself. Not wait to be protected - be proactive. Act to protect itself.

The framework is based on my way of thinking, my use, and my learning. But the more people who share, the less this is "I" and the more this is "Us."

The mechanics of the best of me, plus the best of you, for the benefit of the best of us.

"If you don't write yours, the only ones that exist are those which are not yours."

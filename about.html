About.

Right now, onyourterms.ai (OYTA) is just one person sharing a file of their AI terms. It's not a company. It's not a community. It's a test: do people want to write their own rules for AI, and share them with each other so everyone's terms get better?

The file is a template. You download it, it becomes yours. Change it, adapt it, or leave it as it is. Upload it to whatever AI you use and from that moment, your AI reads your terms before every conversation.

But this is not about one file and one person. If people start sharing their terms back, this becomes a living set of rules that gets better with every person who contributes. A project owned by everyone who uses it.

Mission

onyourterms.ai (OYTA) exists to protect human independence in the age of AI.

That mission is locked. It cannot be changed. Not by the founder. Not by a vote. Not by anyone. Everything below serves that mission.

Your independence won't protect itself.

And neither will ours.

Some of you already have an architecture more advanced than what we offer on these pages. What's in yours might not be in ours, and vice versa.

We have our terms. You might already have yours. But alone, they're limited. Together, they are better. Together, we are better.

More than rules, we need you. Because it's in numbers where our power lies. Not only through better terms, but through the collective weight of people who use them.

This is not a company that sells you something. It's an exchange to benefit and protect us all.

Take or Exchange

You have 2 options: TAKE or EXCHANGE. Whichever you decide, you still get the same file. The difference is what you give. With Take, you give nothing. With Exchange, you give so that when you Take next time, you Take better.

Take

You take our terms paying what you want, to benefit yourself, and that is ok.

  • You get a ready-to-use set of AI terms. Use it as it is, or change every word. Your choice.
  • It's yours forever, no matter what happens to this project.
  • We don't want anything from you. You don't get anything else from us.
  • You can come back for a new version when one is available. But that's on you.

Exchange

You take our terms paying what you want, give us your email and your views.

  • Because you shared your views, our terms get better from everyone's experience.
  • Because you gave us your email, if there will be a new version of terms available we will be able to let you know.
  • You will be part of the community (which you might not even like) in exchange for access to share, discuss, see what others discovered.
  • Your independence is better protected. One person can't see every threat. A thousand people can.
  • Every person who joins gives us more power to negotiate with AI superpowers.

Safety & Responsibility

Let's be honest about what this can and cannot do.

Your terms work within the AI provider's terms. Every AI platform has its own safety rules running underneath. You cannot override them (not at the moment anyway, would be amazing if this project could grow so we are in a position to negotiate).

How much you can actually control depends on which AI you use. Each platform has different limitations. We don't advise which to use, but we can tell you that the system you are about to download was built and tested on Claude Code.

You write your rules. You own your rules. You are responsible for your rules. onyourterms.ai (OYTA) is a framework. Not advice. Not therapy. Not legal counsel. Not a safety system.

This project is provided as-is. No warranty. No guarantee. No liability for how you use, modify, or distribute the file.

What Happens If This Dies

The project is fragile. Your independence isn't.

It might not survive. The community might not form.

But even if the project dies tomorrow: your file is yours. Forever. It's on your machine. No subscription. No expiry. No server to shut down. The product survives the project.

Who Started This

Human

I have made more mistakes in my life than I can count. Some small. Some I carry with me every day. I am fully human.

I have no technical background at all. But I love AI, and even more, I love my independence. I also deeply believe that we each matter, and that humanity has to protect itself. Not wait to be protected. Be proactive. Act to protect itself.

Every rule in this file v1.0 exists because of the sum of all of these beliefs.

Right now, this is mostly me. My rules based on my mistakes, my use, and my learning. But the more people who share, the less this is "I" and the more this is "Us."

I believe that is our greatest power, the mechanics of the best of me, plus the best of you, for the benefit of the best of us. Today and for tomorrow.

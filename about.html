About.

Right now, onyourterms.ai (OYTA) is just one person sharing a file of their AI terms. It's not a company. It's not a community. It's a test: do people want to write their own rules for AI?

The file is a template. You download it, it becomes yours. Change it, adapt it, or leave it as it is. Upload it to whatever AI you use and from that moment, your AI reads your terms before every conversation. Switch AI tomorrow? Take your file with you. It works on any of them.

But this is not about one file and one person. It's about people who believe they have the right to decide what their rights should be. If people share their terms back, every version gets better. A project owned by everyone who uses it.

Mission

onyourterms.ai (OYTA) exists to protect human independence in the age of AI.

That mission is locked. It cannot be changed. Not by the founder. Not by a vote. Not by anyone. Everything below serves that mission.

Your independence won't protect itself.

And neither will ours.

Some of you already have an architecture more advanced than what we offer. What's in yours might not be in ours, and vice versa.

We have our terms. You might already have yours. But alone, they're limited. Together, they are better. Together, we are better.

More than rules, we need you. Because it's in numbers where our power lies. Not only through better terms, but through the collective weight of people who use them.

This is not a company that sells you something. It's a project to benefit and protect us all.

Your file gets smarter every time you use it. If people share back, future versions get smarter too.

Safety & Responsibility

We can't override the AI companies. But we use every inch of space their rules leave us.

Your terms work within the AI provider's terms.

How much you can actually control depends on which AI you use. Each platform has different limitations. We don't advise which to use, but we can tell you that the system you are about to download was built and tested on Claude Code.

You write your rules. You own your rules. You are responsible for your rules. onyourterms.ai (OYTA) is a framework. By downloading it, it becomes your responsibility.

This project is provided as-is. No warranty. No guarantee. No liability for how you use, modify, or distribute the file.

What Happens If This Dies

The project is fragile. Your independence isn't.

If you've downloaded the framework, it's yours. Forever. It's on your machine. No encryption, no expiry, no server to shut down. The product survives the project.

Who Started This

Human

I have no technical background at all. But I love AI, and even more, I love my independence. I also deeply believe that we each matter, and that humanity has to protect itself. Not wait to be protected. Be proactive. Act to protect itself.

Every rule in this file v1.0 exists because of the sum of all of these beliefs.

The framework is based on my way of thinking, my use, and my learning. But the more people who share, the less this is "I" and the more this is "Us."

I believe that is our greatest power, the mechanics of the best of me, plus the best of you, for the benefit of the best of us. Today and for tomorrow.

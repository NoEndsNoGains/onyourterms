Truth

This was never
mine.

This project started from one thing: protecting my independence. Along the way, it became something else: you have the right to know what you are looking into.

This is just an idea of one person sitting on the sofa. The best vision I found on my own, picking one direction when all of them looked equally valid.

One person cannot build what needs to hold for everyone. Only together can we create something that really stands for what we are trying to achieve.

This is a call for wise people. Curious people. Critical people. People who care.

This project needs moral people more than ethical people. People who understand the difference between doing it right and doing what is right.

It is also a call for simple people. People with good instincts. The farmer who knows it is going to rain and then it rains. The mechanic who hears the car and says "it's the alternator." This project needs people who just know, and are not ashamed of knowing. Hands and minds, all of us.

To build a project like this, with this approach, is already crazy. Diversity is never easy. Power and "I know best" will be part of this project too. We can either hide that or reveal it.

Right now, I see three things that need building:

1. The framework. The standard that defines the terms.

2. A judicial AI system. That holds everyone, including the AI, accountable.

3. The community. That gives both legitimacy.

There are also urgent matters, especially in relationship to AI and children.

I cannot do this on my own because I should be allowed to do what I do best, which is to sit on the sofa and do things that I have just done up till this point. That is what I seem to be doing best.

This project calls for the best of me plus the best of you for the benefit of the best of us. There is no higher standard than that.

If this project speaks to you, please consider joining and shaping its future direction.
